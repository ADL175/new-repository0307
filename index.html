About David Lim

Origin Story

I was born in Seattle, Washington. I am the first American born person in my family and I am the oldest of three boys. My younger brothers are Peter and the youngest is Michael. We are about 1.5 years apart.

Childhood

I grew up in the Redmond area, where I went to school at Benjamin Rush Elementary. We lived very close to the school and we walked to school everyday. My parents still own the same house.

Teenage Years

I later went to Rose Hill Jr. High and later Lake Washington High School. While attending school, I played football, wrestling, and rugby. My favorite was wrestling because it was a test of one's skill and resolve against another's.
Rugby was my favorite team sport, because it was very dynamic and every player had a significant roll in advancing the ball.

Adulthood

After finishing high school, I did odd jobs here and there. Later, I signed up and joined the Army in 2004. I served four years in Savannah, GA. I met my beautiful wife, Jennifer, in Savannah.

After my time in the Army, I came back to Washington with Jennifer. I was employed in social work for several years. During this time, we had two amazing girls, Avery 4-years-old, and Zoe 1-year-old. I finished my four year degree and decided I wanted to learn a new skill, software development. I quit my job and started at school at Code Fellows!
