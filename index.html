Origin Story

I was born in Seattle, Washington. I am the first American born person in my family and I am the oldest of three boys. I grew up in the Redmond area, where I went to school at Benjamin Rush Elementary. We lived very close to the school and we walked to school everyday. My parents still own the same house.

My Earlier Years

I later went to Rose Hill Jr. High and later Lake Washington High School. Later, I signed up and joined the Army in 2004. I served in three combat deployments to Iraq and Afghanistan. I served a total four years in Savannah, GA. While in the Army, I met my beautiful wife, Jennifer, in Savannah. Click below to learn more about the unit I was in!

Present

After my time in the Army, I came back to Washington with Jennifer. I was employed in social work for several years. During this time, we had two amazing girls, Avery 4-years-old, and Zoe 1-year-old. I finished my four year degree and decided I wanted to learn a new skill, software development. I quit my job and started at school at Code Fellows!
