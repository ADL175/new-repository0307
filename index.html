Origin Story

I was born in Seattle, Washington. I am the first American born person in my family and I am the oldest of three boys. I grew up in the Redmond area, where I went to school at Benjamin Rush Elementary. We lived very close to the school and we walked to school everyday. My parents still own the same house.

My Earlier Years

I later went to Rose Hill Jr. High and later Lake Washington High School. Later, I signed up and joined the Army in 2004. I served in three combat deployments to Iraq and Afghanistan.
